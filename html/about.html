What I learned in those early days shaped me: structured conversations, adaptability, and understanding how to turn rejection into fuel for progress.

It’s not just about providing a product — it’s about creating an experience that connects with people. That’s what inspired my shift to tech. I started learning web development to scale what I already loved — solving problems and improving systems.

Fast forward, and I began working with small businesses, helping them optimize operations, increase efficiency, and leverage data to make informed decisions. Every time I saw inefficiencies, I thought, “This could be better.” That’s the mindset I bring to the table: identifying opportunities to innovate and create solutions that move businesses forward.

What excites me about opportunities in tech is the chance to bring together my non-traditional, hands-on experience with my love for tech, data, and e-commerce.
